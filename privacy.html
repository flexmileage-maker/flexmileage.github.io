Privacy by Design

At FlexMileage, we believe your movement is your own business. Our application is built on the core principle of Privacy by Design. This means your data stays on your device, is never sold to brokers, and is never shared with third parties without your explicit, manual intervention.

Data Collection

No Account Required

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers. The app functions entirely without an account.

Local Processing

Location data for mileage tracking is processed in real-time locally on your device hardware. It is never transmitted to our servers.

Storage & Security

phonelink_lock

On-Device Trip History

All your logged trips, business classifications, and mileage history are stored in an encrypted local database on your smartphone. We have zero access to this database.

cloud_done

Optional Cloud Sync

If you choose to enable backups, FlexMileage uses your personal Google Drive account. This data goes directly from your phone to your personal cloud storage. It never touches our infrastructure.

block

Zero Third-Party Influence

  • check_circle No Advertisers: We do not include any ad networks or behavioral targeting systems.
  • check_circle No Trackers: We do not use Google Analytics or similar user-tracking SDKs.
  • check_circle No Selling: We do not sell or lease your mileage data to insurance companies or data brokers.

Your Control

Instant Deletion

Uninstalling the FlexMileage app immediately and permanently removes all local data stored on your device.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, export (CSV/PDF), and delete your data at any time through the in-app settings menu.

Contact Support

Have questions about your data? Send us a message and our privacy officer will respond within 48 hours.
