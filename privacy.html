Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

FlexMileage ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

What We Collect

Nothing. FlexMileage collects no personal data. We have no servers, no user accounts, and no analytics.

Location Data

Location permission is required to track mileage. All location data is processed and stored exclusively on your device. We never receive, transmit, or access your location data.

Google Drive Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable Google Drive backup, your mileage data is sent directly to your personal Google Drive account using your own Google credentials. FlexMileage does not receive or store a copy of this data. It is stored in a private, app-specific folder in your Drive that only FlexMileage can access.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party for any purpose, including advertising.
