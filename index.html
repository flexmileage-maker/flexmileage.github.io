Request Data Deletion

FlexMileage stores all trip data locally on your device — not on our servers. To delete your data, simply uninstall the app from your phone. This permanently removes all trips, settings, and locally stored information.

If you used Google Drive backup:

Google Drive → Menu → Settings → Manage apps → FlexMileage → Delete app data.
